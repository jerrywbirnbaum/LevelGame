Boomerang(^)
Moves two squared forward until it hits the end of the level then moves two squared backward until the start the begins again
Does not move if there is a piece where it would move to
Hits the squares directly in front and behind it

CoinPiece(o):
Does not move
Awards a point when the player moves on top of it

FlagPole($):
Does not move
Advances the player when the player moves on it

MudPuddle(8):
Does not move
Does not implement GamePiece
Does not interact

SpikePiece(#):
Does not move 
Kills when the player moves on it

UfoPiece(@):
Moves to a random unoccupied square
Kills the player if they are 2 squares away
